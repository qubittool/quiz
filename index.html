Frequently Asked Questions

What personality quizzes are available?

We offer 5 unique quizzes: SBTI Personality Test (27 types), 16 Tech Personality Test (based on MBTI), AI Mental Age Test, Humanity & Aliveness Test, and Workplace Animal Persona. Each quiz explores different aspects of your personality.

How long do the quizzes take?

Most quizzes take between 2 to 10 minutes depending on the number of questions. The AI Mental Age Test is the quickest at around 2 minutes, while the 16 Tech Personality Test is the most in-depth at about 10 minutes.

Are these quizzes scientifically accurate?

Our quizzes are designed for fun and entertainment, but they are inspired by real personality psychology frameworks such as MBTI and behavioral psychology. They offer meaningful insights while keeping the experience enjoyable.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. All quiz processing happens entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or upload any of your answers or results to any server. Your privacy is fully protected.

Can I share my results?

Yes! Each quiz generates a beautiful, shareable result card that you can download or share directly to social media platforms like Twitter, Facebook, and WeChat.
